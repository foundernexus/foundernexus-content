## LinkedIn, Court Voice

The established executive peer-advisory model is genuinely good: a broad group of leaders across industries, a coach, a relationship you stay in for years. For a lot of executives it's the right home.

FounderNexus is built for a narrower job. Not executives across every kind of business. Venture-scale founders specifically, matched by stage, convened around the live decision in front of them.

Different tools. Know which one your problem actually needs.

## LinkedIn, FounderNexus Voice

A standing peer group and coach for the long haul is one kind of value. A room assembled around the specific call you're carrying right now is another.

The first is continuity. The second is relevance to this decision.

If your need is a durable executive sounding board across industries, the broad model fits. If it's founders at your exact stage pressure-testing the exact call you're on, that narrowness is the whole point of what we build.
