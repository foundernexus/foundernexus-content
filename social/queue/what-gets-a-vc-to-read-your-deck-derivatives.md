# Social Derivatives

## LinkedIn, Court Voice

A founder polishing slides the night before an outreach push is solving the wrong problem.

The deck can be clean and tight and still land in a pile with three hundred other decks that were also clean and tight.

An investor's inbox is a triage system. The sorting happens before content is read, and it comes down to one thing: who sent it.

## LinkedIn, FounderNexus Voice

Founders usually finish the deck, then look for someone to send it. That order is backward.

The search for who has standing with your target investors takes real time. Doing it under fundraising-deadline pressure produces worse asks and weaker vouches.

Relationship first, deck second, ask last. That sequencing is the actual lever.
